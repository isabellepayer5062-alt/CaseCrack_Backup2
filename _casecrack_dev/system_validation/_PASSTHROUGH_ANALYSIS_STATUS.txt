💡 KEY INSIGHTS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

80/20 Rule
  • Top 3–5 commands typically = 70–80% of traffic
  • Focus hardening effort early for maximum ROI
  • Long tail of 30–50 rare commands = 20–30% traffic

Error Rate Signals
  • <1% error: Safe for immediate migration
  • 1–5% error: Audit first; may need special handling
  • >5% error: High risk; defer or investigate
  
Tenant Adoption Matters
  • Multi-tenant commands: Affects many users → prioritize
  • Single-tenant: Experimental/custom → may defer
  • No-tenant: Dead code → deprecate

Volume Concentration
  • Concentrated (80% in 5 cmds): Easy to prioritize
  • Scattered (evenly distributed): May need different strategy
  • Typical: 70–80% concentration = good for staging
